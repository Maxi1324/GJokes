GJokes

Congratulations, you found GJokes. On this website you can recall lots of funny jokes. A repository is created and the jokes are commited to it. You can get jokes every day. Just creat an account and get started :)

As you can see on the contributions above jokes are send every day to the account of the repository.
Enjoy the funny jokes and your contributionstats.

HOW TO USE

When you register, with your email and passwort, you recive a 12-digit-token to verify. After you logged into your account, you can configure your github settings. Type in your github username, email and repository name. Then you can send jokes to that repository. You can also choose how many you want, by setting a minimum and maximum. By clicking on generate jokes now, you have a more detailed selection, where you can specify how many jokes you want to generate and what the daterange is.
